What Digital Pru is

FractiAI builds the member experience: music, honor passes, nesting guides on the top deck. Digital Pru is the name for the technical truth layer: compile rules, edge onboarding, and honesty boundaries. Same house. You are reading the engine room now.

Intelligent pipes (data-free center)

We keep the server as data-free as we can. The center is not a warehouse of profiles, playlists, or listening history. It is a set of intelligent pipes: route, verify, sign, merge catalogs. The heavy life stays on your edge.

  • Your pass — signed token on your device after honor boarding (Venmo, PayPal, Cash App). No Stripe. No member database we mine.
  • Your taste — playlists in browser storage; tracks from static catalog plus upload pipes.
  • Our pipes — boarding JWT, optional single-stream heartbeat, catalog API. Small handlers, not a platform brain.

That cuts complexity, cost, and raises sovereignty: you hold the pass; we do not own your listening graph.

What runs the sound

Tracks merge from the server catalog (static manifest plus optional uploads). Previews play until the Machote honor pass ($16.18/mo) unlocks full playback on the Sovereign Player. Exports use the same Fair Exchange rails as boarding.
