How to play (plain speak)

Goal: Light up every zone on the board into a just-right zone. The best helper of the table wins — most fair trades and surges handled.

  • Someone else’s zone — fair trade: offer chips (and optional adult add-on if on). They may tip some chips back.
  • Big solar surge — ground yourself or you pause until you help the active player (honor reset).
  • Nested helpers — table master wakes sun / fair-trade / surge helpers one at a time (EGS φ = 1.618 nesting). Not a flat peer swarm.
