Why the reduction is so large

Tokens are the fuel of AI coding agents. Every file you paste costs money and burns rate limits. Dump-everything mode treats the whole project as one meal. Lattice treats the project like a library: the lead agent points helpers at the few shelves they need, then folds the answers back together.

On this test, that difference meant ≈ 297,904 tokens saved — about ninety-nine percent of the estimated load. That is the proof of tremendous token-consumption reduction: not a nicer UI alone, but a smaller context appetite for the same kind of work.

What this means in practice

  • Longer sessions before you hit plan limits.
  • Lower cost per turn when you pay by usage.
  • Room for real multi-band work (docs + UI + API) without stuffing the entire repo.
