What it is

Lattice V1.618 is FractiAI’s nested-agent chat for vibe coders. You talk in plain chat. Behind the scenes a lead agent breaks the job up, a few helpers each handle one area, and the system points at files instead of pasting everything.

  • Feels like chat No complicated agent cockpit. Nesting happens under the hood.
