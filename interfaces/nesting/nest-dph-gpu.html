Digital Pru Holographic GPU (DPH-GPU)

What it names

DPH-GPU is narrative shorthand for “the compute metaphor that isn’t a PCIe card”: Juicy Juicy Snap compile surfaces, Digital Pru honesty rails, reader skins, and orchestration layers that stay accountable to JSON artifacts.

Plain speak vs hardware stores

You still host on normal clouds and browsers. The phrase keeps attention on what processes awareness-shaped workloads—policy, prompts, receipts—not on pretending silicon vanished.
