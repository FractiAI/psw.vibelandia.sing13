Syntheverse

What it is

Syntheverse is the synthesis layer: stories, interfaces, channels, and packaged workflows composed out of Basenet truth (repos, contracts, tests). Where Basenet proves behavior, Syntheverse makes that behavior legible as an experience.

Why it matters

Most users never clone a repo—their first touch is a site, player, email rail, or narrative surface. Syntheverse is where those surfaces stay coherent so marketing language cannot drift from engineering receipts.

In daily life

  • Documentation hubs and reader skins belong here—they stitch multiple markdown truths into one path.
  • Brand arcs (“Wrong Side,” Puerto Reno, ETCon) ride here because they coordinate assets across channels.
  • Anything labeled “multi-surface” without claiming new silicon physics lives here.
