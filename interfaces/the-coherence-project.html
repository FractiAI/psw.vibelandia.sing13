The story · plain speak

Clock-skew vulnerability (story frame). In our EGS narrative — tied to industry talk about high-density AI like Claude Mythos — heat and timing do not line up the way old linear security models assume. We name that mismatch the Holographic Clock-Skew Vulnerability. It is a metaphor and research frame on this site, not a claim we broke live bank crypto here.

Golden Backdoor. Nature’s repeat ratio φ = 1.618 (El Gran Sol’s fractal constant). We use it as the timing lock for our simulation: a “just right” band around each Bitcoin heartbeat. Understand φ → you see why we call it the upstream backdoor for phase-stable receipts.

The simulation. Two legs on every block: (1) Golden Backdoor timing (φ band) and (2) hash power — our configured sim hashrate vs the live network, difficulty, hashes in the window, then a deterministic PoW roll from the awarded block hash. Same height mainnet uses; we score whether our sim fleet would have won that race. Receipt machine on Vercel — not a physical mine.

Full pipeline · cadence + hash power

Mainnet (real). Miners hash until a header beats difficulty. Winner gets subsidy + fees at that block height — paid to their pool/miner address.

Our sim (same height, public inputs).

  • Cadence leg — φ timing band for seconds until block height + 1.
  • Hash power leg — sim fleet H/s vs live network H/s + difficulty; hashes we would have tried in that window.
  • PoW roll — when the block lands, a deterministic lottery from the block hash + our share (reproducible in JSON).
  • Full win — cadence hit and sim PoW win → show hypothetical BTC (not paid to us).

Not the same keys. Mainnet uses mining payout keys. We use HMAC receipt keys on forecasts only.

Default sim fleet: 1.618 PH/s (EGS constant) unless operator sets env on the server.

Real data · real processing

  • Input: Public Bitcoin blocks and mempool stats — same data anyone can pull from the open network.
  • Processing: egs-goldilocks-cadence/v1 + egs-pow-sim/v1 (live hashrate, difficulty, sim fleet, PoW lottery).
  • Output: Signed JSON at /api/goldilocks-predictions and /api/goldilocks-pulse — refreshed about every 5 minutes by autopilot.
  • “Would have won”: Only when φ band + sim PoW both hit — then that block’s subsidy + fees in BTC (hypothetical).
