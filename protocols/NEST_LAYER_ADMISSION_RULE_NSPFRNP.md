# Nest Layer Admission Rule — NSPFRNP (Goldilocks Gate)

**Protocol:** NSPFRNP
**Status:** ⚡ ACTIVE
**Applies:** Any new layer proposed for the QUESTFEST nesting ladder (Layer 10 and beyond), and any new top-deck feature slot.

---

## The rule (irreducible)

A layer earns a rung on the ladder **only** when all three exist:

| Gate | Requirement | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **1. One nest guide** | A single plain-language page under `interfaces/nesting/nest-*.html` — one screen, what it is, why it's nested here | `nest-goldilocks-beehive.html` |
| **2. One honesty row** | A voice-tier declaration: what is 🜛 mythic, what is ⚙ operational, what is 📐 verified about this layer | Honesty boundary table in the guide |
| **3. One seed hook** | Something another edge can attach to or fork: an API, a template in `seed/`, a manifest, a contact protocol | `/api/catalog` · seed kit · Old School Protocol email |

No exceptions. A layer with two gates is a draft, not a rung.

## Goldilocks gate (not too much, not too little)

- **Ladder stays readable:** if adding a rung makes the ladder exceed 13 layers, a lower layer must merge or move to the parent edge first.
- **Front door stays three doors wide:** new layers nest under Listen, Read, or Build — they never add a fourth door.
- **One featured slot:** the top deck carries exactly one "latest" feature card. The previous one moves to the press archive the day the new one lands.

## Squeeze on entry (one in, one archived)

When a new layer or paper lands:

1. Register it (whitepaper registry / ladder / llms.txt).
2. Move the superseded item to the legacy index (`docs/LEGACY_INDEX.md` or `interfaces/look-under-the-hood-legacy-catalog.html`).
3. Re-run the smoke test on the three doors.
