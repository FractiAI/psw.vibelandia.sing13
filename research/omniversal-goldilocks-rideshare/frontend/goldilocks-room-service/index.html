How it works

  1. Email Pru to join — as purveyor, valet, concierge, or guest. One email, one human answer. No forms, no app store, no account.
  2. Accepted? You're in the channel — we place you in the WhatsApp group for your channel, where requests broadcast and members accept.
  3. Broadcast what you want and your tip offer — food, beverage, merchandise, or other services. A valet or concierge accepts what fits. No algorithm, no surge math, no queue.
  4. All else handled offline — procurement, delivery, and payment ride the established rails (Venmo · PayPal · Cash App), honor system, Fair Exchange.

The floors below are courtesy minimums ($9 runs · $16.18/hr · $161.80/day). Your tip offer above the floor is what makes the exchange sing — and what makes a node say yes.
