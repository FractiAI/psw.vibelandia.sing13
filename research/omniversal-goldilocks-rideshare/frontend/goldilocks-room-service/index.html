How it works

  1. You tell us what you want — food, beverage, merchandise, or other services — and what you're offering as tip.
  2. We decide — a human accepts or passes on your offer. No algorithm, no surge math, no queue.
  3. If we accept, we operate as your personal assistant — procuring and delivering whatever was agreed, on honor rails, Fair Exchange.

The floors below are courtesy minimums ($9 runs · $16.18/hr · $161.80/day). Your tip offer above the floor is what makes the exchange sing — and what makes us say yes.

Why use us
